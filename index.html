However, this was the first time that he had encountered such a situation. Thus, he wanted to know exactly what sort of thing had suddenly appeared. Thus, Chu Feng used his mental power to communicate with it. She wanted to use his mental power to dissect it and see what exactly this mysterious object was. "Wuuwaa ~ ~ ~" However, the moment Chu Feng touched it, not only did her mental force instantly scatter, she even felt that her head was about to explode. The pain that engulfed his entire body made Chu Feng, who was completely unprepared, let out a painful wail. In that instant, not only did Chu Feng's consciousness return to his body, she also felt that her brain and his entire body were paralyzed. With a "putong" sound, she fell onto the ground. Chu Feng seemed to have become severely ill, like she had just woken up. Not only was her mind not clear, even her body was not very sensitive. When Chu Feng finally regained her senses, her eyes revealed a look of fear, as though there was a lingering fear in her heart. "What the hell is that?" Chu Feng could not help but ask.
